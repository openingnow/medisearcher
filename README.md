1. Identify medications (Korean) and active ingredients (ATC names; alphabet lowercase)
2. Review potential drug interactions:
   - ["Flockhart table"](https://drug-interactions.medicine.iu.edu/MainTable.aspx)
   - KFDA drug label (by LLMs; results are subject to error)

Colors
- Blue - Ingredients were identified and KFDA drug label was reviewed
- Cyan - Ingredients were identified, but the KFDA drug label was not reviewed (yet)
- Green - Medication was identified, but the ingredient is not in identification list
- Yellow - More than one medication or ingredient can be matched
- Red - No medication or ingredient can be matched
